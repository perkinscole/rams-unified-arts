Homework Page Setup Guide

Connect your Google Classroom to the RAMS UA website
RAMS Unified Arts
We have a new homework page on the Unified Arts website that automatically pulls assignments from Google Classroom. Once you complete this quick setup, your posted assignments will appear on the site for parents and students — no extra work needed from you each week!

Part A: Add Cole as Co-Teacher

1 Open Google Classroom and go to each class you want on the homework page
2 Click People at the top
3 Next to "Teachers," click the person+ icon to invite a teacher
4 Enter perkinsc@holliston.k12.ma.us and click Invite
5 Repeat for each class (all grade levels)
Why? The system needs co-teacher access to read your posted assignments. Cole will not modify anything in your Classroom.

Part B: Configure Your Courses

1 Visit the Teacher Setup Page (link below)
2 Enter your school email and display name (e.g., "Mrs. Hebert")
3 Select your subject from the dropdown
4 Click "Load My Courses" — your classes will appear
5 Check the boxes next to the classes you want included
6 Click "Save Configuration" — you're done!

Checklist

  • Added perkinsc@holliston.k12.ma.us as co-teacher on all my classes

  • Entered my email and display name
  • Selected my subject
  • Loaded and checked my courses
  • Clicked "Save Configuration"

Questions? Email Cole Perkins at perkinsc@holliston.k12.ma.us or stop by the CS room.

How it works: The system automatically checks Google Classroom every morning at 6 AM and updates the homework page. You don't need to do anything beyond your normal Classroom posting!
