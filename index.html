What is Unified Arts?

Unified Arts at Robert Adams Middle School offers students in grades 6–8 a well-rounded experience across Art, Band, Chorus, General Music, Computer Science, Health, Physical Education, and Wellness 2.

These are year-long courses — students receive one final grade at the end of the year. When checking PowerSchool, look for the Y1 column.

  • Full-year courses across 8 disciplines
  • Standards-based and letter grading
  • Concerts, performances, and showcases throughout the year
  • Extra help available from every teacher
