Need Help?

Step 1: Co-Teacher Access

Add perkinsc@holliston.k12.ma.us as a co-teacher on each Google Classroom course you want on the homework page.

Step 2: Fill Out the Form

Enter your school email, display name, and subject above. Then click "Load My Courses" to see your classes.

Step 3: Select & Save

Check the courses you want included, then click "Save Configuration." You're done!

When Does It Update?

The homework page refreshes automatically every morning at 6 AM. You can also ask Mr. Perkins to trigger a manual refresh.
